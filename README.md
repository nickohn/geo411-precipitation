# geo411-precipitation
R analysis of precipitation data for Buffalo and San Diego

R and calculations by hand were used to analyze the precipitation data for Buffalo and San Diego between the years of 1940 and 2025.

Buffalo's precipitation data was found to be a normal distribution with a mean of 38.82779 and a standard deviation of 5.43462. San Diego's data is skewed to the right, with a mean of 9.908605 and a standard deviation of 4.224307.

RStudio running with XQuartz on MacOS was used.
